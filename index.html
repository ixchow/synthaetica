Synthætica

A LD48 #47 game by Jim McCann / TCHOW llc

Click on the colored squares in the timeline at the bottom to control the ship, collect keys, and get to the star at the end.

Ship Controls: The first row (red) moves right, the second row (green) moves left, the third row (yellow) activates a shield, and the fourth row (purple) gives a burst of speed in the direction indicated by the first two rows.

Space plays/pauses.

You can move the looped portion of the timeline as well as the current playhead by click-dragging the grey rectangles at the top of the timeline.

Collect keys to free the loop to move to other portions of the timeline.
